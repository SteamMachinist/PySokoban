Sokoban

The goal of the puzzle is to push all the boxes onto the goals.

Listed below are the specific rules to solve a Classic puzzle:

  • Only one box can be pushed at a time
  • A box cannot be pulled
  • The Sokoban cannot walk through boxes or walls
  • The puzzle is solved when all boxes are on the goals

Controls for playing Sokoban:

  • WASD keys for movement
  • R for reset
  • B for back

Sokoban:

  • You are in control of Sokoban
  • The Sokoban can move up, down, left and right by using the WASD keys on the keyboard

Wall:

  • Walls surround a puzzle and Sokoban cannot walk through them
  • Walls make up the layout of the puzzle

Box/Goal:

  • Boxes can be pushed by Sokoban
  • Boxes must be placed on all the Goals in the puzzle
